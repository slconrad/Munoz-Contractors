About Munoz Contractors

“At Muñoz Contractors, excavation and dirt work are not just services—they’re our craft. Based in East Texas, we specialize in professional land clearing, excavation, and grading solutions designed to get your construction project started the right way.”

At Munoz Contractors, excavation and dirt work are not just services—they’re our craft. Based in East Texas, we specialize in professional land clearing, excavation, and grading solutions designed to get your project started the right way. From raw, overgrown acreage to perfectly prepared house pads and driveways, we focus on doing the critical groundwork that ensures everything built on top is safe, stable, and built to last.

We proudly serve homeowners, builders, ranchers, and small commercial clients across the East Texas region. Our team understands the unique challenges of local soils, terrain, and drainage—from red clay and sandy loam to low-lying, water-prone areas—and we tailor every project to the land in front of us. Whether you need a house pad cut and compacted to spec, a driveway or private road built, right-of-way and fence line clearing, site grading, or pond and drainage improvements, we bring the right equipment and the right plan to every job.

Professionalism is at the core of how we operate. That means clear communication, honest estimates, and showing up when we say we will. We take the time to listen to your goals, walk the property with you, and explain the best options for your budget and timeline. Safety, respect for your property, and attention to detail are non‑negotiables on every project, whether it’s a small residential pad or a multi-acre land clearing job.

As a local East Texas business, we’re invested in the communities we serve. Our reputation is built on word‑of‑mouth, repeat customers, and work you can see and stand on—literally. If you’re planning to build, improve access, reclaim overgrown land, or solve long‑standing drainage problems, Munoz Contractors is here to help you turn raw ground into a solid foundation.
